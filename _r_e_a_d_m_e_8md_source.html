9 ## Dependencies
10 - InFactory (https://github.com/STEMLab/InFactory) : To generate IndoorGML
11 - OSM (https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Main_Page) : OSM wiki
12 - rapidxml (http://rapidxml.sourceforge.net) : To read COLLADA(*.dae) file
13 ## Building
14 
15 Dependencies should be pre-installed in your environment.
16 - Linux
17 ```

19 cd build
20 cmake ..
21 make
22 ```
23 - Windows
24 ```
25 mkdir build

28 mingw32-make
